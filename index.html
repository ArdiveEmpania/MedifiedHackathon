Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our service

How do I search for medicine prices?

Simply type the name of your medication in the search bar at the top of the page. You can also filter by location to find prices at pharmacies near you.

How often are prices updated?

We update our prices daily from partner pharmacies. However, for the most accurate and current pricing, we recommend contacting the pharmacy directly before visiting.

Can I order medicines through MediFind?

Currently, we provide price comparison and availability information. When you find a medicine you need, we'll direct you to the pharmacy's website or provide their contact information for ordering.
